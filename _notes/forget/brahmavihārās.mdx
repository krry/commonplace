# Brahmavihārās

## 1. metta

- [[मैत्री]] maitrī
- loving-kindness
- sharing one's good will
- far enemy: blame
- near enemy: fear
- "the golden rule"

## 2. karuṇā

- [[करुणा]] karuṇā
- compassion
- accepting another's suffering as one's own
- far enemy: shame
- near enemy: guilt
- results from mettā
- "turn the other cheek"

## 3. mudita

- [[मुदिता]] mudita
- sharing joy
- empathy, sympathy, compersion
- far enemy: envy
- near enemy: spite
- your joy is our joy
- unlike Schadenfreude, which is joy in another's pain

## 4. uppekha

- [[उपेक्षा]] upekṣā
- equanimity, universal impartiality
- far enemy: greed
- near enemy: apathy
- any being is all beings
