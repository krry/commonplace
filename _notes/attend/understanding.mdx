# Understanding

Insights interwoven into a canopy
to shield us from the beating sun
and lend us rest, on backs not feet

Tree canopies make lovely umbrellas

The bottom rung on [[the-learning-ladder]]
Although we rarely reach it first
as we step down into [[the-well-of-being]].
