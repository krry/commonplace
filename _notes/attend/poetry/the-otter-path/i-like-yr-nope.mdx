# I like yr nope

"I'll take you on vacation when we die. I was built to protect"
those like me
who are here to love
and since we'll always be
let's travel indefinitely
as the winds blow us
into the waves
the raging flames
of void soil

So I like yr nope
it tastes like this
void we dance of
