# Church - The Game

## game pieces

* figures: Lego clergy members
* board: Church, steeple, people
* cards: describe the actions taken by Catholic clergy
** corruption
** political intrigue
** sexual indiscretions
** war mongering
** martyrdom
** sainthood
** pacifism
** scholarship

## game play

* object: survive the corruption of the Church and become Pope
