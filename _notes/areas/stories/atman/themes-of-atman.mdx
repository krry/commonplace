# Themes of Atman

- Trilaksana
  - three marks of existence via Buddhism
  - anicca, dukkha, anatta
  - nirvana
- Purusarthas
  - four proper aims of a life via Hinduism
  - dharma, artha, kama, moksha
  - samsara, karma, sat-chit-ananda (सच्चिदानन्द)
- simulation argument
- evolution and extinction
- qualia - subjective experience via sense and perception
- determinism? - "it is as it always would have been"
