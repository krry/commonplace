# Events of Atman

## Evolution

- new species
- a new mutation survives
- the code gets an update

## Cataclysm

- the end of many lives
- mass transmigration

## Extinction

- the end of a species
- failure

## Unity

- moksha - knowledge of self, _atma jnana_
- nirvana - liberation from suffering
- the Brahman awakens/the singularity
- kalpa or aeon closes

## Transmigration

- souls relocate according to karma
