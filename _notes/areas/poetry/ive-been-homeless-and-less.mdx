---
title: Homeless and Less
time: 2021-03-08 14:12:29
tags: poem, terse
---

I've been homeless and less
attached to a place in space
on a date with eternity
not in a date of modernity

I've been home
less and less on my own
more and more in the zones
only known to the mind
-less and free-spirited

I've been home
I am
and I still am

# Homeless and Less
