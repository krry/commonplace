# a matrimonial blessing for dustin and ashley

- v1
  - May you daily dive
  - the infinite pools
  - of love within
  - the hearts you share
  - and keep a map
  - of the wonders you find
  - of the fears you face
  - of the peaks you climb
  - of the steps you dance
  - and the pain you erase
  - the paths to grace
  - joys you crave
  - ways to shine
  - and grow with grace.
- v2
  - May you daily dive
  - the endless pools
  - of love within
  - the hearts you share
  - Wonders found
  - fears you face
  - peaks you climb
  - pains erased
  - joys you crave
  - steps you dance
  - ways to shine
  - & paths to grace
  - logged herein or floating free
  - can trace the map
  - that guides us thru
  - the rainbow realms
  - of lasting bliss.
