# Wooman

Behold me
For **I** am Wooman
Vessel of love
Deliverer of wiggles
Stork employer

You may recognize me
from such episodes as
Your First Blink
and
Who Am I, Really?

For I am Wooman
Protector of Goo
Captor of Essence
Release your grasp
and harken my spelling

When your sucker gets puckered
Wooman is near
in the adjacent vicinity
carrying areolae
ready to nurse your thirst

When your pucker gets blupped
Wooman appears
to wipe away your fear
some stain their sheets
without healing a rear
