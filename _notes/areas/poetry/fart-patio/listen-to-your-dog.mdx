# Listen to your dog

Gaze into those baby browns
Wiggle into wag
Nuzzle puppy love
Everything's mmkay

No hurry in her scurry
Just being knowing enjoying
Flopping ears remix our hears
Soft tongues invite faces, ears

Tails tell true tales
