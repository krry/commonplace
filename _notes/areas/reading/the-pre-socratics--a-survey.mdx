# The Pre-Socratics: A Survey

Before we begin our journey into the annals of western philosophy, a quick note on time. For all we know, time is an illusion. It is always now, and each of us is always here. As such space may be illusory as well. With that in mind—acknowledging that the only time one can be sure to exist is ***now***—all of the dates referenced herein are counted from the moment you read them. I have asked one or more of our illustrious Internet of computers to programmatically generate the years for you, dear reader, so that you will have a more intuitive sense of the *anciency*[^1] of the annals we are exploring.

# Notions

## Ananke
Necessity, blind astral determinism, irrational plough

## Thanatos
Death, demise, oblivion, cold inactive stasis

From PK Dick's *VALIS*: Thanatos can assume any form it wishes, even Eros, such that the "love" driving you might be death in a mask.

## Eros
Affinity, attraction, appeal

## Logos
Word, idea, thought, intellect
