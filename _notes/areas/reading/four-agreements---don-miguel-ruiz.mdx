# Four Agreements

## via Don Miguel Ruiz
1. Be impeccable with your word.
2. Don't take anything personally.
3. Don't make assumptions.
4. Always do your best.

## As affirmations:
1. I speak my truth.
2. I make up my mind.
3. I embrace ignorance.
4. I love wholly.

## As positive directives:
1. Speak only truth.
2. Respect our minds.
3. Always be asking.
4. Always do your best.
