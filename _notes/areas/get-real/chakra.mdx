# Chakra

In Sutra Yoga there are held to be seven chakras, or primary energy nodes, in the human body

| position | seat      | mantra | sanskrit      | petals         |
| -------: | --------- | :----: | ------------- | -------------- |
|        7 | Crown     |   AH   | Sahasrara     | 1000 petals    |
|        6 | Third Eye | OM/AUM | Ajna          | 48 or 2 petals |
|        5 | Throat    |  HAM   | Vishuddha     | 16 petals      |
|        4 | Heart     |  YAM   | Anahata       | 12 petals      |
|        3 | Belly     |  RAM   | Manipura      | 10 petals      |
|        2 | Sacrum    |  VAM   | Svadhishthana | 6 petals       |
|        1 | Root      |  LAM   | Muladhara     | 4 petals       |

`4 + 6 + 10 + 12 + 16 = 48`
`96 / 2 = 48`
