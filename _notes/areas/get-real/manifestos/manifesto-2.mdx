# Manifesto 2

The Church of Material Scientism has us. We have been unwittingly reciting its doctrine our whole lives. You may not consider yourself a scientist, but its effects are ubiquitous, far-reaching; it has seeped into our collective unconscious and bled to the forefront of our suffering cerebra. There's little purpose in denying it, though I have a feeling you will, at least until you've broken through the protective membrane that has healed over and sealed in the infection. Let's recite the prayer together. I promise it will be familiar.
