# Manifesto 3The Revolution is coming. It will be a gentle Revolution of love and connectedness. We will start with the young, or rather, they will start without us. For when one becomes conscious of the multitude of others who are at their core no different than oneself, one starts to wonder whether there are others at all. Eventually the programming we have all been steeped in will not apply, for the fresh young Minds will be exposed to the truth more often and sooner than the lies.
