# a MeetingRoom API: the verbs

## 1. Hush
* Message: holding your tongue
* Nonverbal: finger over lips, repetitive actions like blinking or hair play
* Verbal: active listening, permissive affirmatives, "okay"

## 2. Shush
* Message: please hold your tongue
* Nonverbal: finger over lips with targeted eyes, grand reposturing, seat shifts, standups
* Verbal: "Hold it", "But...", etc.

## 3. Recap
* Message: here's what we've agreed on
* Nonverbal: open palms up, standup, conspicuous smiling
* Verbal: "So...", rhetorical uncertainty ("Lemme see if I have this right...")
