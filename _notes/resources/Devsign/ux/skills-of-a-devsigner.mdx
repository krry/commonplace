# Skills of a Devsigner

A devsigner paints in working code. She sets up an experience stand at the intersection of supply and demand. She knows her target users and her team's resources and objectives. She casts a wide net in a deep sea and comes up with sparkly unicornfish.

The skill set of a devsigner draws from the core competencies of designers, developers, and engineers.

## Design skills:
* vector illustration
* iconography
* typography
* web layout
* interface wireframing

## Development skills:
* build systems
* integration testing
* unit testing
