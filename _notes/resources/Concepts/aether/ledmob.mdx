# LEDmob

Join the mob by personalizing and configuring your wearable smart LEDs. The signup process gathers some relevant data from moblings to predict the suitable "mode" or "theme" of their LED

## Products
- Bike tube valve caps - kinetic powered blinking or glowing led
- Moto or bike helmet with accelo turn signals, brake lights, headlights, light sensor, patterns
- Glove controller for wearable matrix
