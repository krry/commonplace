# How to buy a train ticket
0. Go to station
1. Choose your destination
2. Move aside
3. Grab a ticket
4. Move along
5. Pay your fare
6. Move onward
7. Follow the signs
7. Board your train
8. Sit down
9. Wait for arrival
10. Leave the train

6. Wait for arrival
