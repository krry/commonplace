# Olivella

> The DAO (decentralized autonomous organization) behind the Olivella marketplace and the Pimento Coin

## Thesis
The value of your money should formally be a function of your seniority, connectedness, and performance (activity and reliability) in the marketplace.

## Factors
1. Vouchitude
	* upper tier - 6 or more vouchers
	* middle tier - 2 to 5 vouchers
	* lower tier - up to 1 voucher
2. Reputation - average/aggregate satisfaction rating
3. Popularity - percentile of likes/followers
4. Affinity - number and degrees of network connections
5. Proximity - degrees of nearness to another party
