# Blue eyes

Apparently 8-10% of humans have blue eyes
this recessive trait originated 6 to 10,000 years ago with one individual whose genetic mutation preventing melanin to be produced in the Iris Is now present in so many people that it expresses, despite being a recessive gene, in 8 to 10% of them.
