# Growing Up Vs Waking Up

A perpective or model of personal development offered by Ken Wilber:

- in the West we focus on growing up
  - Maslow's hierarchy from survival to actualization
  - Jung's belief that our goal is individualisation
- in the East we focus on waking up
  - 8 limbs of yoga sutra - Patanjaji and Ashtanga
  - Buddhist stream entry
  - Zen detachment
