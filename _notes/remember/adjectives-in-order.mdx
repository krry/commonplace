# Description Generator

Akin to a madlib, but you only have to come up with one noun.

## Flow

1. Enter a noun and hit **Roll**.
2. Eight random adjectives are generated.
3. **Reroll** at will.
4. Background image of the noun?
5. Select the adjectives you want
6. Hit **Copy**

## Natural order of adjectives

0. Quantity
1. Opinion
2. Size
3. Age
4. Shape
5. Color
6. Origin
7. Material
8. Purpose
