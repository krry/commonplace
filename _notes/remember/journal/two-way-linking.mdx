# Mutualinks

First of all, I want a sexier name for this.

Here are some options:
- bidirectional links
- symmetrical links
- two-way Links
- symlinks (taken to mean symbolic links)
- mutualinks

The last of which goes well with:
- internalinks
- externalinks

Mutualinks it is!
