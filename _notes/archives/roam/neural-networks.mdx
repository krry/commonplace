# Neural networks

Brain regions aren't innately specialized. If you rewire your eyes to the auditory cortex, it will learn how to see.

## Neuronal structure in the brain
cell body/nucleus
inputs - dendrites
output - axon
axons send messages to the dendrites of other neurons
