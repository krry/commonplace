## Food

We'll want to take the guesswork out of our diet.

### Be careful about:

- caffeine < 200 mg/day
- Salmonella on the surface of meat, in cold cuts, raw eggs, raw sprouts
- Listeria on raw fish
- E.coli on unpasteurized milk, cheese, juice
- Toxoplasma on produce and cat litter
- Hg in fish like tuna, swordfish
- organ meat which can lead to vitamin A toxicity

### Indulge in:

- yogurt - protein, calcium, fat, probiotics
- legumes - folate (B9) for first trime
- beta-carotene - sweet potatoes, carrots
- omega 3 - pacific wild caught salmon
- vitamin D - salmon
- choline - eggs
- dark greens (broccoli, kale, spinach)
- iron - meat?
- vitamin C (oranges, bell peppers) - helps iron absorption
- berries - low glycemic, vitC
- whole grains - oats, quinoa
- avocados - fatty acids for brain/skin, potassium to prevent muscle cramps
