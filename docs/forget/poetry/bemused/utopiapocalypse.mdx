# Utopiapocalypse

"Endless thanks!"
Famous last words
on the final night
when the stars link arms
take a bow and blow out
a dusty kiss as
existence consciousness bliss
holds its breath.

What the black holes concealed
became suddenly clear
when we saw once again
to know is to remember.
