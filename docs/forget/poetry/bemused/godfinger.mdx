# Godfinger

Thumbs up, okay
ready, set, flow
Dive in with Poseidon
Swim free and tread steady

Point to smite
with Zeus' lightning
an insurance plan
for disobeyed commands.

Middle fingers up
Send somebody down
to lonely Hades.

A hug from Aphrodite
lasts forever ringing
finger four in love divine.

Pinky's small but fierce,
pulsing with the atomic fury
of Ares incarnate.

A fist of characters
at your fingertips
or rather round their hilts.
